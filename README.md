# Data-Professional-Survey-Breakdown-
What if the best way to learn about your dream career is to analyze it? 📊

I've always been curious about a career in data—questions like:

Which programming languages are most in demand?
What skills matter the most?
How do salaries vary?
What does the job market actually look like?

Instead of searching for answers one by one, I decided to do what a Data Analyst would do: analyze the data myself.

Using Excel, Power Query, Power BI, DAX, KPIs, measures, slicers, and interactive visualizations, I built a dashboard that uncovers insights into the data analytics profession and the factors shaping it.

But the biggest lesson wasn't just the insights—it was the process.

I realized that data cleaning and feature engineering consume nearly 80% of the project, and getting them right is far more important than creating visuals. A well-designed dashboard is only as good as the quality of the data behind it.

This project strengthened my technical skills while reinforcing a valuable mindset:
Every meaningful business question can be answered with data—provided you ask the right questions and prepare the data well.

I'd love to hear your thoughts or feedback on the dashboard!
